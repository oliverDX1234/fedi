WHY JOIN US

3 Reasons to join GMSPP

  • Your fast track to getting your medical license in Germany

    We guarantee optimal and time-saving preparation to obtain a German medical license.

  • Expand your medical knowledge and develop your skills

    We work closely with healthcare institutions and hospitals in Germany to offer you practical experiences.

  • Benefit from our experienced counsellors that will guide you from day one to settle into a new environment in Germany

    Our focus is to unlock your potential by overcoming administrative and cultural challenges in Germany.

Your profile:

    • You are on the last semester of your medical school, or you hold a degree in medicine after studying for at least six years at a university (including 12 months practical training).
    • You are interested in German culture and are motivated to learn the German language.
    • You have passion and motivation to continue your studies as a medical specialist in Germany.
  • Prepare for an interview

    Upon receiving your application, you ‘ll be invited for a one-on-one interview from Germany. The interview aims to understand better your motivations, goals, expectations, character, and personality.

  • Application decision

    Your final admission decision will be made according to your high school grades, motivation letter, and interview. You will receive the result via email within seven days after your interview.
