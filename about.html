ABOUT

OUR STORY

FEDI (Future Education Institute) is a German and Singapore-based education institute that shapes and creates successful pathways for aspiring students from Southeast Asia to Germany. We aim to support your personal and professional development and bridging the cultural gap so you can unlock your full potential and become a future-ready person.

Our approach is to connect you with the real world by embracing challenges as opportunities to grow and improve your knowledge and skills, while at the same time we will assist you from day one in identifying important resources, building strategies, and executing them.

OUR GOAL

FEDI exclusively partners with international ranked universities, hospitals, clinics, start-ups, and companies in Germany to offer you Bachelor program, Medical program and Innovation program. Our FEDI program is more than just a degree; it is a life-changing experience and your pathway to a successful career through our innovative community, excellent business education, company training phases (internships), workshops, and case studies.
