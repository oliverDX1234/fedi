ABOUT US

FEDI (Future Education Institute) is a German and Singaporean education institute that paves the way for aspiring students from Southeast Asia to have a successful academic and professional career in Germany.

FEDI will connect you to the real world and help you learn how to turn challenges into opportunities. We aim not only to support your personal and professional development but also to help you integrate the German culture and life.

MORE THAN JUST A DEGREE

  • FEDI exclusively partnered with high-ranked universities, hospitals, clinics, companies, and start-ups in Germany to offer you the best educational programs.

    We highlight the synergy of theory and practice to give you an insight into real-world business situations.

    Our FEDI program is more than just a degree; it is a life-changing experience. With our innovative community, excellent business education, internships, and workshops we guarantee you a prosperous future.
