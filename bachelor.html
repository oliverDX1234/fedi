Overview FEDI Bachelor Program

Bachelor’s degree: Bachelor of Arts (BA) of International Business Administration

Duration of the program: 9 semesters

The Program is divided into two parts

Part one online: The Foundation program

The 18-month preparation program is equal to the German university entrance qualification or Studienkolleg. Once you have completed the program, you will be granted admission to our BA program in Germany.

The foundation program will shape you to have the right qualifications for joining our undergraduate program in Germany. It provides a broad introduction to management, innovation, economics, and intercultural courses. In addition, it prepares you to achieve your German B1 Goethe Certification and TOEFL IBT 90.

This program is integrated with the BA program; thus, your study place in Germany is secured.

Language of instruction: English and Indonesian

Part two in Germany: Bachelor’s in International Business Administration

The BA program blends academic studies and professional experience in the German corporate world. The program is spread over six semesters (three years), with four theory semesters at our campus and up to two company practice semesters starting in the 5th semester.

The unique three-day study program (Monday to Wednesday) will allow you to work part-time and gain your professional experience.

Three specializations are available for you to choose: Innovation, Entrepreneurship, and Digitalization

The undergraduate program is taught in English and German

Admission

We value your talent, motivation, and dedication. You will get an individual appointment for a personal application procedure. Quality over Quantity is our commitment; thus, we carefully select 25 students per semester to join our program. Studying in small groups will allow you to develop your problem-solving, interpersonal, presentational and communication skills.

Expand your global perspective with us through a three-step admission process:

  • Apply online

    To process your application, please submit the following documents to us at application@fedicampus.com

    • High School Diploma or the last two school reports if you have yet to graduate
    • Curriculum Vitae
    • Motivation letter (max. two pages)
  • Prepare for an interview

    Upon receiving your application, you ‘ll be invited for a one-on-one interview from Germany. The interview aims to understand better your motivations, goals, expectations, character, and personality.

  • Application decision

    Your final admission decision will be made according to your high school grades, motivation letter, and interview. You will receive the result via email within seven days after your interview.
